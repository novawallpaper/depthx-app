Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how DepthNova ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Google Account: When you sign in with Google, we collect your name, email address, and profile picture.
  • Custom Wallpaper Orders: If you upload images for custom wallpaper creation, we collect those images and any instructions you provide solely to complete your order.
  • Payment Information: Payments are securely processed by Razorpay. We never store your card, UPI, or banking details.
  • Usage Information: We may collect information such as downloads, favorites, browsing activity, and feature usage to improve our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide our services.
  • To personalize your experience.
  • To process Premium subscriptions.
  • To fulfill custom wallpaper orders.
  • To improve website performance.
  • To prevent fraud and abuse.
  • To communicate important updates.

3. Custom Wallpaper Privacy

Images uploaded for custom wallpaper requests remain private and are only accessible by you and our team for order fulfillment.

4. Data Sharing

We never sell your personal information. We only share information with trusted service providers required to operate our services.

  • Google Sign-In
  • Firebase

  • Google AdSense
  • Razorpay
  • EmailJS

DepthNova uses cookies and similar technologies to improve your browsing experience, analyze traffic, and display advertisements.

Third-party vendors including Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your previous visits to this website or other websites.

Google's advertising cookies enable personalized advertisements where applicable. You can manage your advertising preferences through your Google Ads Settings.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

7. Your Rights

  • Request access to your personal data.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your account and personal data.
  • Disable cookies through your browser settings.

DepthNova is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
